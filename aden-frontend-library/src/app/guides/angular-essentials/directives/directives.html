Components are great, but sometimes they are overkill. If I just want an image to have a "lazy load" effect, or an input field to automatically focus when the page loads, I don't want to wrap them in a
<my-wrapper> component.

That's why I love Directives. They act like "plugins" or "mixins" for your HTML. I write the logic once (e.g., "Make background colorful on hover") and can then attach this capability to any element in my appâ€”whether it's a <div>, a <button>, or another component.
